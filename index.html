Meme Booth
Spotted a rising trend of people recreating meme poses in photo booths, but sharing required manually editing the meme and photo strip together before posting. After hearing a friend describe the friction firsthand, I built Meme Booth: a web app using real-time pose detection to snap a photo the moment the user matches each respective pose, then layers them together for an instant, shareable result.
